Fallout 4 Survival Build Guide: The Wasteland Commando

This guide is for Fallout 4 Survival Mode players who want to avoid Power Armor, Sneak, and Melee. The Wasteland Commando build centers around using rifles, staying mobile, and leveraging chems and healing to survive. With a focus on mid-to-long-range combat, durability, and sustain, this build allows you to handle a variety of threats from a safe distance.

SPECIAL Stats (Starting Build)

• Strength – 3: Allows carry weight improvements and access to Armorer
• Perception – 5: Enables Rifleman for semi-auto rifles
• Endurance – 6: For Life Giver and Chem Resistance
• Charisma – 3: Access to Lone Wanderer for survival boosts
• Intelligence – 6: Unlocks Medic, Chemist, Scrapper
• Agility – 4: Good for Action Boy and eventual Gun Fu
• Luck – 1: Low initial investment (not a crit build)

Key Perks to Focus On

Combat Focus:

• Rifleman – Core perk for semi-auto rifles
• Commando – Optional if switching to automatic weapons
• Gun Nut – Enables weapon upgrades
• Awareness – Target resistances in VATS

Survivability:

• Life Giver – Passive HP and regen
• Toughness – Flat DR bonus
• Adamantium Skeleton – Avoid limb damage
• Solar Powered – Sunlight-based healing and stat boost
• Refractor – Energy resistance

Utility & Sustain:

• Lone Wanderer – Huge for survival
• Medic – Better healing from stimpaks
• Chemist – Enhances duration of chems
• Scrapper – More resources for crafting
• Action Boy – Faster AP regen

Recommended Weapons and Armor

Weapons: • Combat Rifle – Strong semi-auto or auto option
• Laser Rifle – Great mod potential and ammo availability
• Hunting Rifle – Good early-game choice for sniping
• Deliverer – Use if you dip into pistols
Armor:
• Heavy Combat Armor – Ideal for defense
• Ballistic Weave on clothing underneath
• Focus on mobility and damage resistance

Survival Tips

• Use chems like Jet, Psycho, and Med-X in tough fights
• Sleep to save and manage fatigue
• Cook and eat food regularly for buffs
• Maintain distance and use cover – avoid brawls
